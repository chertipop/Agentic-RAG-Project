Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries by automating tasks and improving efficiency.

Machine learning is a subset of AI that focuses on learning from data and improving performance over time.

Deep learning is a subset of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers.

AI is used in healthcare, finance, education, customer service, manufacturing, and transportation.

Benefits of AI include automation, faster decision-making, personalization, and cost reduction.

Risks of AI include bias, data privacy concerns, job displacement, and overreliance on automated systems.

Future trends of AI include human-AI collaboration, responsible AI, automation, and job transformation.
